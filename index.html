    About Us

    Home salon believes in beauty with a conscience. We have created a salon that offers the highest quality hair, makeup and skincare services with professional stylists at your doorstep. Just a few clicks away, customers can enjoy simple but quality-guaranteed salon and beauty services at the comfort of the customer’s own home. HOME SALON specifically caters to local businesses and provides alternative business solutions for local stylists to reach out to their customers. Local stylists can create business profiles and customers will be able to look for specific styling services that meet their preference and purpose. Customers can narrow down their search by service category, price range, year of professional experience, service location and special discount offer.

    Covid-19 Policy

    A MESSAGE TO OUR CLIENTS

    We all need to work together to make this successful, and you are a huge part of that. All of our staff are certified in proper sanitation and safety protocol. This is a list of all of the things that we’d like you to do to help all of us…
    • If you are feeling sick or have been in contact with anyone who has been sick, please reschedule.

    • Upon stylist arrival, customer’s temperature will be taken.

    • It is mandatory for both stylists and customers to sanitize their hands.

    • Wearing masks is necessary.

    • Customers must provide health status from the past 2 weeks upon appointment booking.

    • Stylists must not accept booking if they are experiencing flu-like symptoms; and as a courtesy, they should report to the web-administrator for records.

    • Service site occupancy must be limited to household members.

    • Both parties reserve the right to terminate the service if either party fail to comply to the safety policy.

    Our Site Policy

    APPOINTMENT POLICY

    We recommend that you reserve appointments in advance in order to receive your preferred date, time and service provider for your services.

    CANCELLATION POLICY

    Our staff is in great demand, so please be respectful to their time and that of other guests by honoring your commitment to be here for your reservation. We require at least 24 hours when cancelling an appointment.

    FOR YOUR PROTECTION

    We regret that we cannot be responsible for loss or damage to personal articles including clothing or accessories. Please keep valuables with you during your appointment.

    GRATUITIES

    In appreciation of outstanding service, gratuities may be given at your discretion.

    VIOLATERS WILL BE BANNED FROM OUR WEB APPLICATION SYSTEM.
